Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

UniGo respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the UniGo mobile application.

2. Information Collected

UniGo may collect or store limited information necessary to provide its features, including:

• Academic data such as attendance, assignments, and schedules entered by the user.
• Basic account information if authentication services are used.
• Device-related information required for notifications and app functionality.

3. How Information Is Used

The information stored by UniGo is used solely to provide the core functionality of the app, including task management, reminders, and academic scheduling.

UniGo does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

4. Data Storage

User data may be stored locally on the user's device or through secure backend services used by the application to maintain functionality and synchronization.

5. Notifications

UniGo may send notifications related to assignments, attendance reminders, and exam schedules. These notifications are part of the core functionality of the app.

6. Third-Party Services

UniGo may use trusted third-party services such as authentication or cloud storage providers to support app features. These services operate under their own privacy policies.

7. Data Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect user data. However, no digital platform can guarantee complete security, and users should use the application responsibly.

8. Children's Privacy

UniGo is intended for students and general users. The application does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the application after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer through the social links provided within the app.
