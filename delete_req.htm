Account Deletion Request

Request Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your UniGo account and all associated data, you can request account deletion by sending an email to the address below.

reportthis.kavy.dev@gmail.com

Steps to Request Deletion

1. Send an email from the address associated with your UniGo account.
2. Use the subject line "UniGo Account Deletion Request".
3. Include your UniGo account email address in the message.

Data That Will Be Deleted

When your request is processed, the following data will be permanently removed:

• Your UniGo account
• Assignment records
• Exam schedules
• Notes and stored academic data
• Any other associated user data

Deletion requests are typically processed within 7 days.
