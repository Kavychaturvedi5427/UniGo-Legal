About UniGo

UniGo is a student-focused productivity application designed to help students stay organized and manage their academic responsibilities efficiently.

The app allows users to track attendance, manage assignments, organize exam schedules, and receive timely reminders to stay on top of academic tasks.

UniGo aims to simplify student life by providing a clean and minimal interface that helps students focus on what matters most — their studies.

Features

• Attendance tracking
• Assignment management
• Exam schedule organization
• Smart reminders and notifications
• Simple and student-friendly interface

Developer

UniGo was developed by Kavy Chaturvedi as a project focused on improving student productivity through technology.
